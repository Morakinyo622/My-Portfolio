About Us

BeckieTee Global is a Lagos-based brand with a passion for preserving Nigerian food culture. Our journey began with a simple belief: food made with natural ingredients, care, and flavor matters. We craft our Spiced Powdered Pap with yellow corn and aromatics like ginger, bay leaf, and cloves — delivering warmth, nostalgia, and health in every spoonful.

Our values are anchored on:

  • Quality ingredients — no preservatives or artificial additives
  • Taste that honours tradition
  • Health & well-being
  • Community & sustainability
